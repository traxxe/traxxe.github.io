Apartment life

Safe pod. What I love about this living pod.

This space is clean and no smell and high ceilings and big windows with built in shades. I am not on the ground floor so feel safer, yet close enough to be able to view people passing by to the stairs. My corner unit has a lot of windows for a one bedroom and I look at the parking lot. That might not sound interesting but it really is. I get really amused when a giant moving van passes by my window because I only see the very top and they remind me of massive ocean freighters passing by a condo window down at the beach.
